About

Mayralee Marinez is a first generation Salvadoran woman who is currently studying New Media at Molloy College with aspirations to become a Social Media Manager. Mayralee was born and raised on Long Island, NY and graduated from Freeport High School in 2019. After graduation, Mayralee plans to work with management companies and/or small businesses to help them build their online profiles and manage their online platforms to help promote their businesses.
